ABOUT ME

An insight into my world

Born in Mumbai on 22nd February 1998 the world has fascinated me right from the beginning! I believe I am a global citizen. The goal of my life right from the start has been learning! Be it travelling, trekking, writing, phtography, singing, tinkering or reading I have always learned from my experiences and gained greater insights to life.
